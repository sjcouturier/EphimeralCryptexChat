CRYPTEX
System Manual
v1.0 · CLASSIFIED · EYES ONLY
// 01. What Is This
CRYPTEX is a turn-based, end-to-end encrypted messenger where each message exists exactly once. Read it — and it's gone forever. The server never sees your plaintext. Not even the admin can.
// 02. How Encryption Works
01 On first launch your device generates a unique ECDH P-256 key pair. Your private key never leaves your browser.
02 When you open a channel, your browser derives a shared secret with your partner via ECDH key exchange. The server cannot compute this.
03 Messages are encrypted with AES-256-GCM in your browser before being sent. The server stores ciphertext + IV only.
04 After you decrypt a message it is permanently deleted from the server. No copy remains anywhere.
// 03. Turn Order
→ One message is in play per channel at a time. You cannot send again until your partner has read and replied.
→ YOUR TURN means a transmission is waiting for you, or you may compose the first message.
→ You'll receive a push notification when it's your turn. The channel lives until you both decide to close it.
// 04. First Setup
01 Sign in with GitHub. A key pair is generated automatically on first visit.
02 Tap + to open a new channel. Enter your partner's GitHub username.
03 Enable notifications when prompted — this is how you'll know it's your turn.
